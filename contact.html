Frequently Asked Questions

What courses are offered at Sir C R Reddy College of Pharmaceutical Sciences?

We offer B.Pharm, M.Pharm, and Ph.D programs in various pharmaceutical disciplines including Pharmacology, Pharmaceutics, Pharmaceutical Chemistry, and Pharmacognosy.

What are the admission requirements for B.Pharm program?

Candidates must have passed 10+2 examination with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology/Mathematics as compulsory subjects with a minimum of 50% aggregate marks. Admission is based on merit and entrance examination.

Does the college provide hostel facilities?

Yes, we provide separate hostel facilities for boys and girls with all necessary amenities including Wi-Fi, mess, and 24/7 security.

What are the college timings?

The college operates from 9:00 AM to 4:30 PM, Monday to Saturday. Library and laboratories have extended hours for student convenience.

Does the college provide placement assistance?

Yes, we have a dedicated placement cell that organizes campus recruitment drives, industry interactions, and provides career guidance to students.
