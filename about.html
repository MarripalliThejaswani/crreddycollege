About SIR C. R. Reddy College of Pharmaceutical Sciences

SIR C. R. Reddy College of Pharmaceutical Sciences, established in 2007, is located in West Godavari, Andhra Pradesh, and stands as a reputed institution dedicated to excellence in pharmaceutical education and research. The college is affiliated with Andhra University, Visakhapatnam, and is recognized for its commitment to nurturing competent pharmacy professionals.

It offers a well-structured academic curriculum comprising one undergraduate program (B.Pharm) and three postgraduate programs (M.Pharm) across various specializations, making up a total of four pharmacy courses. With a team of qualified faculty, modern laboratories, and a student-centered learning environment, the college ensures holistic development through academic rigor, hands-on training, and research exposure.

SIR C. R. Reddy College of Pharmaceutical Sciences continues to strive toward academic distinction and innovation in the field of pharmacy.
