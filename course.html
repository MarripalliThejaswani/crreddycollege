Undergraduate Programs

Bachelor of Pharmacy (B.Pharm)

A four-year undergraduate program that provides comprehensive knowledge in pharmaceutical sciences.

Duration: 4 Years
Eligibility: 10+2 with PCB
Intake: 100 Seats
  • Semester 1-2: Basic Sciences & Pharmaceutical Chemistry
  • Semester 3-4: Pharmacology & Pharmaceutics
  • Semester 5-6: Pharmacognosy & Clinical Research
  • Semester 7-8: Advanced Topics & Internship
Apply Now

Doctor of Pharmacy (Pharm.D)

A six-year program including five years of study and one year of internship or residency.

Duration: 6 Years
Eligibility: 10+2 with PCB
Intake: 30 Seats
  • Year 1-2: Basic Medical Sciences
  • Year 3-4: Pharmaceutical Sciences
  • Year 5: Clinical Pharmacy & Practice
  • Year 6: Internship/Residency
Apply Now

M.Pharm in Pharmaceutics

Advanced program focusing on drug formulation, delivery systems, and pharmaceutical technology.

Duration: 2 Years
Eligibility: B.Pharm
Intake: 15 Seats
  • Semester 1: Advanced Pharmaceutics
  • Semester 2: Novel Drug Delivery Systems
  • Semester 3: Biopharmaceutics & Pharmacokinetics
  • Semester 4: Research Project
Apply Now

M.Pharm in Pharmacology

Duration: 2 Years
Eligibility: B.Pharm
Intake: 15 Seats
  • Semester 1: Advanced Pharmacology
  • Semester 2: Clinical Pharmacology
  • Semester 3: Toxicology & Pharmacovigilance
  • Semester 4: Research Project

Doctoral Programs

Ph.D in Pharmaceutical Sciences

Research-oriented doctoral program with specializations in various pharmaceutical disciplines.

Duration: 3-5 Years
Eligibility: M.Pharm/M.S
Intake: As per UGC
  • Year 1: Coursework & Research Proposal
  • Year 2: Comprehensive Exam & Research
  • Year 3-5: Dissertation Research
  • Final: Thesis Submission & Defense
