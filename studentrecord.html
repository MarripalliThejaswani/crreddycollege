Eligibility Requirements

Documents and criteria needed for enrollment

B. Pharm Eligibility

  • 10+2 examination with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology/Mathematics
  • Minimum 50% aggregate marks in qualifying examination
  • Valid score in entrance examination (if applicable)
  • Age limit: 17 years as on 31st December of admission year

M. Pharm Eligibility

  • Bachelor's degree in Pharmacy (B. Pharm) from recognized university
  • Minimum 55% aggregate marks in B. Pharm
  • Valid score in GPAT or equivalent entrance examination
  • No age limit for admission

Pharm. D Eligibility

  • 10+2 examination with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology/Mathematics
  • Minimum 60% aggregate marks in qualifying examination
  • Valid score in entrance examination (if applicable)
  • Age limit: 17 years as on 31st December of admission year

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common admission queries

What are the eligibility criteria for B. Pharm?

Candidates must have passed 10+2 examination with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology/Mathematics with a minimum of 50% aggregate marks. The age limit is 17 years as on 31st December of the admission year.

Is there an entrance examination for admission?

Yes, admissions are based on merit in the qualifying examination and performance in the entrance examination conducted by the college or state authority.

What is the fee structure for different courses?

The fee structure varies for each course. Detailed fee information is provided during the counseling session. We also offer scholarships and financial assistance to deserving students.

What are the career opportunities after completing pharmacy courses?

Pharmacy graduates have diverse career opportunities in community pharmacy, hospital pharmacy, pharmaceutical industry, research and development, regulatory affairs, and academics.

Does the college provide hostel facilities?

Yes, we provide separate hostel facilities for boys and girls with all necessary amenities including Wi-Fi, mess, and 24/7 security.
